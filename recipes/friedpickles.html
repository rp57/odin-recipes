Fried Pickle Recipe

Description

I love Fried Pickles and you will too after trying this recipe. Great while watching ball and great with a couple beers!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon vinegar-based hot pepper sauce
  • 3/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon seasoning salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 jar of dill pickle slices
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  1. Combine 2 eggs, 1/4 cup of flour, buttermilk, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, cayenne pepper, seasoning salt, and garlic powder into a large bowl
  2. In a seperate bowl combine cornmeal, 2 cups flour, salt, and 3/4 teaspoon black pepper
  3. Preheat oil in deep fryer to 365 degrees F. Drain pickles and then dip into milk mixture and dredge in flour mixture.
  4. Deep fry until golden brown.
  5. Drain and Serve!
