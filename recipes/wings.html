Buffalo Wing Recipe

Description

I love Chicken Wings and you will too after trying this recipe. Great while watching ball and great with a couple beers!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 10 chicken wings (scale ingredients up for more)
  • 2 cups of vegetable oil for frying
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup hot sauce
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  1. Whisk together flour, paprika, cayenne pepper, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Toss wings in flour mixture. Put wings on a baking dish and refrigerate for an hour.
  3. Add 1 inch of oil to a deep skillet. Heat oil to 375 degrees F.
  4. At the same time combine butter, hot sauce, pepper, and garlic powder into a pan on low heat and stir until blended.
  5. Fry coated wings in the oil for 10-15 minutes until they begin to turn brown.
  6. Toss wings in sauce mixture and serve!
