Onion Ring Recipe

Description

I love Onion Rings and you will too after trying this recipe. Great while watching ball and great with a couple beers!

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Vidalia or sweet onion
  • 1 quart of frying oil
  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 3/4 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 1/8 teaspoon seasoned salt
  1. Slice onion into 1/4 inch thick rings. Heat oil in a deep fryer to 365 degrees F. Place a wire rack over a sheet of foil.
  2. Prepare breading station. One dish of flour, baking powder, and salt. Another of the whisked egg and milk. Lastly one with bread crumbs.
  3. Dip each ring into the flour mixture, turning until fully coated.
  4. Put ring i egg mixture and turn until coated. Shake gently to remove excess liquid.
  5. Place ring in bread crumbs and turn until coated. Tap to remove excess.
  6. Place rings on wire rack as you go.
  7. Deep fry 3-4 rings at a time until golden brown (2-3 minutes). Drain on paper towel.
  8. Sprinkle with seasoning salt and serve!
